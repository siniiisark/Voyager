Popular Space Missions

Apollo 11

Launch Date: July 16, 1969

Mission Type: Moon landing

Apollo 11 was the first manned mission to land on the Moon and return safely to Earth. It was conducted by NASA and carried astronauts Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin, and Michael Collins.

Voyager 1

Launch Date: September 5, 1977

Mission Type: Interstellar probe

Voyager 1 was launched to study the outer planets and is now the most distant human-made object from Earth. It continues to send data back from beyond our solar system.

Curiosity Rover

Launch Date: November 26, 2011

Mission Type: Mars exploration

Curiosity is a car-sized rover that landed on Mars to explore the Gale Crater. Its primary goal is to assess whether Mars ever had conditions suitable for microbial life.

Hubble Space Telescope

Launch Date: April 24, 1990

Mission Type: Space observatory

The Hubble Space Telescope has been orbiting Earth for decades, capturing breathtaking images of distant galaxies, nebulae, and stars. It has revolutionized our understanding of the universe.

New Horizons

Launch Date: January 19, 2006

Mission Type: Pluto flyby

New Horizons is a NASA mission that conducted the first close-up flyby of Pluto, providing detailed images and data of the dwarf planet and its moons.

James Webb Space Telescope

Launch Date: December 25, 2021

Mission Type: Space observatory

The James Webb Space Telescope is designed to look further back in time than ever before, observing the formation of the first galaxies and the atmospheres of exoplanets.
